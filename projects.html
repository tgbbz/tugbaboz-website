Research & Development Projects

Teaching AI in Elementary and Middle Schools

AI Literacy

This project focuses on developing sustainable, integrated approaches for teaching artificial intelligence concepts in elementary and middle school settings. It involves co-designing curriculum with educators, providing professional development, and supporting teachers to embed AI into core subjects such as science, math, and engineering particularly in rural schools.

Teaching AI to Pre-Service Elementary Teachers

AI Education

This project explores how elementary teacher candidates can meaningfully engage with AI concepts through hands-on activities, robotics, and classroom integration. It aims to prepare future teachers to embed AI literacy into core subject areas and foster age-appropriate AI understanding in elementary school settings.

STEM Strong

NSF DRK–12

Funded by the National Science Foundation (DRK–12), STEM STRONG provides supports to teachers and promotes science and engineering in elementary classrooms.

My Home, My Health

NIH SEPA

Supported by the NIH SEPA program, this initiative empowers students to explore disease ecology concepts in their communities.
